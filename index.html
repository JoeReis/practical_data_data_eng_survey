About This Survey

Overview

The 2026 State of Data Engineering Survey captures insights from data professionals worldwide about their tools, practices, challenges, and predictions for the year ahead.

Methodology

Responses 1,101
Collection Period December 2, 2025 to January 10, 2026
Distribution Newsletter, LinkedIn, X/Twitter
Target Audience Data Engineers, Architects, Analytics Engineers
